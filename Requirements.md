# AI Student Companion - Requirements

## Functional Requirements
- Students can submit mood and issues anytime.
- Daily mood and stress analysis 
- Weekly emotional trend data collection 
-  helping in coping and study suggestions.
- Motivational and supportive messages.
- Admin dashboard with user analytics.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Privacy-first and anonymous system.
- usable across multiple institutions.
- Secured data storage.
- Simple and accessible datas
